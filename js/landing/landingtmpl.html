Chess Ladder Home

This web-app serves as a ladder, game reporter, and stat tracker created for the Kearns High Chess Club.

This copy of the web app (hosted on firebase) has a separate database from the original, and is logged in automatically. So feel free to report games here to see how it works.

To use the game reporter, go to the ladder, select the two players who played a game, then click the knight at the bottom right.

The content on this page is also designed to change based on the browser size. Don't believe me? Resize the window, or view it on mobile. See?! Magic!
